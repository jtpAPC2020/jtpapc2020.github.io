About Us

K-Chick N’ Crisp is a student-led venture dedicated to Korean fried chicken at TUP Manila.

Welcome to K-Chick N’ Crisp, a culinary venture driven by the entrepreneurial spirit of fourth-year college students from Technological University of the Philippines (TUP) Manila! We are a group of passionate and innovative individuals who share a common love for Korean cuisine, particularly the mouthwatering delight that is Korean fried chicken.

K-Chick N’ Crisp was born from the shared dreams of friends and classmates who turned their passion for food into a reality despite their academic commitments.

Our journey began as a group of friends and classmates who were united by our shared dreams of creating something special. We decided to turn our passion for food into a tangible reality, and that's how K-Chick N’ Crisp was born. As students, we have dedicated countless hours to perfecting our recipes and culinary techniques, all while juggling our academic responsibilities.

Our commitment to authenticity lies in our dedication to preserving the genuine flavors of Korean fried chicken through meticulous research and experimentation.

What sets us apart is our commitment to authenticity. We believe in staying true to the rich and flavorful tradition of Korean fried chicken, which is why we have painstakingly studied the art of making the perfect crispy, golden chicken. Our secret marinades and sauces are a result of countless taste tests and experimentation to ensure a truly authentic Korean taste that will tantalize your taste buds.
